Privacy Policy for Avios Reminder Chrome Extension

This extension does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information or browsing data.

The permissions used (such as activeTab, tabs, scripting, and storage) are strictly required to provide core functionality:

  • Remind users when they visit Avios-supported sites
  • Allow the user to configure reminder frequency
  • Save preferences locally on the user's browser

No data is sent to any remote server. All data stays on the user's device.

Policy last updated: July 8th 2025
